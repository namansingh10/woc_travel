GRAND HYATT GOA

About this Hotel

This high-end hotel with direct access to Bambolim Beach is 7 km from both the Goa State Museum and Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church, Goa. Elegant, contemporary rooms come with terraces, flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and minibars, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add whirlpool baths, sea views and access to a club lounge; some include dining areas. Amenities include an Italian restaurant, an informal Indian restaurant, an all-day restaurant serving eastern cuisine, plus 2 bars and a pastry shop. There are 2 outdoor pools and an indoor pool, plus a spa with hot tubs, a steam room and a fitness room.
