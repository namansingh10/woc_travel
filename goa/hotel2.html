About this Hotel

In a series of wood cabins surrounded by palm trees on Odxel Beach, this breezy resort is 5 km from the Goa Science Centre and 27 km from Goa International Airport. Featuring wood-paneled walls and ceilings, the cozy cabins have free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as covered terraces with pool or sea views. There's an informal restaurant and a relaxed outdoor eatery offering shaded seating and a bar. Other amenities include an outdoor pool and a kids' playground.
