COUNTRY INN & SUITES

About this Hotel

Set 1 km from Casino Royale Goa, this informal hotel off NH66 is 5 km each from Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary and Miramar Beach. Warm, relaxed rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as tea and coffeemaking equipment. Upgraded rooms add whirlpool tubs. Suites feature river views, and separate living areas with sofas. Room service is available 24/7. Hot breakfast is complimentary. There's a rooftop BBQ restaurant with river views, plus a 24-hour eatery and a bar. Other amenities include a rooftop outdoor pool, a gym and a business centre.
