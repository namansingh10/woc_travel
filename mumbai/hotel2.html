INTERCONTINENTAL

About this Hotel

Situated in Mumbai’s business district, InterContinental Marine Drive boasts views of the Arabian Sea. It offers free parking, a rooftop swimming pool and spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows. Modern décor and hardwood flooring feature throughout the non-smoking rooms in Marine Drive InterContinental. Each well-appointed room is fitted with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and en suite bathroom with a bathtub. Guests can work out at the 24-hour fitness centre or enjoy a relaxing body massage at the spa. The hotel also provides car rental services, while travel arrangements can be made at the tour desk.
