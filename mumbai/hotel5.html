SAROVAR GRAND HOMETEL

About this Hotel

Bordering the Mindspace business district in north Mumbai, this relaxed hotel is 2.9 km from Goregaon train station and 6 km from the Bombay Convention & Exhibition Centre. Modern, warmly decorated rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and work desks, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Room service is available 24/7. Freebies include a breakfast buffet, parking and a convention centre shuttle. There's an airy restaurant serving buffet-style, international cuisine, plus a fitness room, a business centre and a rooftop event space.
