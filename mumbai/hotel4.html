HYATT REGENCY

About this Hotel

A 5-minute walk from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport and a 15-minute walk from the nearest metro stop, this swanky airport hotel is 4.5 km from downtown Mumbai. Posh rooms have elegant decor, marble bathrooms and floor-to-ceiling windows. All come with flat-screen TVs, minibars and free Wi-Fi, as well as safes, iPod docks, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add separate sitting areas. Room service is offered 24/7. Parking is free. Amenities include 2 upscale restaurants, including a 2-story Italian eatery overlooking a garden, plus a bar and a bakery. There's also an outdoor pool, a tennis court, and a spa with a gym.
