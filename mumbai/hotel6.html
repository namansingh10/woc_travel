HOTEL IMPERIAL PALACE

About this Hotel

Set 3 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, this unassuming hotel is 4 km from the ancient Mahakali Caves and 5 km from Versova Beach. Straightforward rooms offer Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemaking facilities; some include sitting areas. Room service is available 24/7. Amenities include a low-key restaurant, a bar and a 24-hour coffee shop, There's also a banquet hall, and a terrace with city views. Airport transfers are available.
