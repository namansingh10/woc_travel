BAMBOLIM BEACH RESORT

About this Hotel

This seafront hotel, set on Siridao Beach, is 7 km from Panjim, the Goa state capitol, and 14 km from Karmali Railway Station. Set in colourful, Portuguese Colonial-style cottages, the simple rooms feature flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms include balconies with tile mosaic floors, and suites provide sitting areas. Kids 5 and under stay at no extra charge. A breakfast buffet is free, and there's an open-air restaurant. Other amenities include beach access and an outdoor pool and sundeck, as well as a fitness room and a game room. Parking is included.
