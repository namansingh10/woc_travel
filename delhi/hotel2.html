HYATT DELHI RESIDENCES

About this Hotel

With Qutub Minar reachable in 4.3 mi, Hyatt Delhi Residences provides accommodations, a restaurant, a fitness center, a bar and a garden. Both WiFi and private parking are available at the apartment free of charge. Hyatt Delhi Residences offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Guests can enjoy the spa and wellness center, organize trips at the tour desk, or rent a car to explore the surroundings. Gandhi Smriti is 6.2 mi from the accommodations, while Rashtrapati Bhavan is 6.2 mi away. The nearest airport is Delhi International, 1.2 mi from Hyatt Delhi Residences, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. We speak your language!
