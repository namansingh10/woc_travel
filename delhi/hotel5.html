THE CLARIDGES

About this Hotel

Located in Lutyens New Delhi, The Claridges, New Delhi, offers rooms with old-world charm and contemporary furnishings. Just 1.9 mi from the city center, it has a temperature controlled outdoor swimming pool and 6 dining options. Free WiFi and parking is available. Wooden-floored rooms feature a satellite flat-screen TV and a pillow menu. Each is well-equipped with a mini-bar and tea/coffee making facilities. Safety deposit boxes and ironing facilities are available for guests convenience.
