SERENE WATERS RESORT GOA

About this Hotel

Set in Ribandar and featuring an infinity pool, 1.7 mi from Panaji Bus stop, Serene Waters Goa offers free WiFi. It includes private parking on site. All units have a seating area where you can relax. Every room includes air-conditioning and a private bathroom. The rooms offer a backwaters view. The Relic of St. Francis Xavier and St. Agustine Tower are 3.7 mi from Serene Waters. Miramar beach is 3.7 mi away. Calangute beach is 8.7 mi away while Uthorda is 11 mi from the property. The closest railway station is Karmali 4.7 mi from the property. Dabolim International Airport is 16 mi from Serene Waters Goa.
