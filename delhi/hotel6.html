THE PARK NEW DELHI

About this Hotel

A luxury hotel in New Delhi’s Connaught Place, The Park New Delhi offers accommodations with modern comforts. It houses Aura Spa and Agni Nightclub, and also features 3 dining options. The property overlooks the 18th Century observatory Jantar Mantar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV and safety deposit box. Each room has a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Certain rooms feature bathtub.
